Palaw

Description

Palaw is very commong in Afghanistan

Its basically rice and meat but the

it comes in diffrent veraities and ingridients

depending on what region of the country its made.

This picture is called Kabuli palaw, as you probably guessed

its from around Kabul area.

The full meal have got more to it but the main focus is the Palaw itself.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Meat
  • Rasins
  • Carrots
  • Almonds
  • Oil
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Spices(Palaw Masala)

Preparation

  1. Put rice in soak beforehand, better if overnight
  2. Fry onions with Meat in oil
  3. Pour some water and salt on the Meat
  4. Let it cook for some time
  5. Fry carrots then put the rasins over afterwards.
  6. When the meat is soft and tender, remove it from the dish
  7. Pour boiling water in the dish and add the rice with the spices
  8. When rice is soft enough and the water tried up
  9. Put the carrot and the rasins on top of the rice
  10. Add the meat that was removed before on the rice
  11. Let it sit for some time on low heat
